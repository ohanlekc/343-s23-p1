The Jeep Yard is an all Jeep salvage yard specializing in 2006 and older Jeeps established in September 2020. We are a family owned business and the owner, Tyler, has been working on Jeeps his whole life. He has a passion for restoring and bringing life back to these old beauties. The Jeep Yard is a place where you can make that happen, and you can find all the difficult to locate Jeep parts for whatever project you are working on. The Jeep Yard is located in Chesterfield, VA so if you are local or in the surrounding areas please make an appointment to browse the yard. We are not a pick and pull and you have to have an appointment to enter the yard, but shipping is also available for customers located farther away. Most shipments have a turn around time of 3-7 business days, depending on the item and on workflow.

Most of the inventory consists of XJ, MJ, ZJ, WJ, TJ, YJ, and a few FSJ parts. Roughly 50 jeeps in the yard at this time, but always looking for and adding new inventory. If you have any inquiries about inventory please fill out “Need a part?” or contact the Jeep Yard through text/telephone and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
